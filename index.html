This Telecommunications Service Agreement (“Agreement”) is entered into as of the Effective Date by and between: Provider: Apex Telecom Services Inc. Client: ___________________________ 1. SERVICES The Provider agrees to supply telecommunications services including voice calling, internet connectivity, data transmission, and optional cloud-based communication tools (“Services”). TERM This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of 24 months, unless terminated earlier in accordance with this Agreement. 3.
